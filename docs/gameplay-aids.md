# Cthulhu Wars Gameplay Aids and Player Resources

A curated reference of player aids, rulebooks, strategy resources, video content, and community materials for Cthulhu Wars. Intended to populate the "Resources" page of the strategy guide site.

**BGG Game ID:** 139976

---

## Official Resources

Resources produced or published by Petersen Games, the game's designer and publisher.

### Rulebooks and Rules Documents

- **Official Cthulhu Wars Rulebook (Final Edition)**
  - Distributed with Onslaught 3 and later printings of the base game
  - Available digitally from the Petersen Games website and as files uploaded to BGG
  - BGG files page: [https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/139976/cthulhu-wars/files](https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/139976/cthulhu-wars/files)
  - Note: The podcast host (Episode 27) confirmed that earlier rulebook printings contained errors; the "final, final" rulebook resolved several long-standing rule disputes. If you have an older printing, cross-reference with the BGG files page.

- **Petersen Games Official Website**
  - [https://petersengames.com/cthulhu-wars/](https://petersengames.com/cthulhu-wars/)
  - Product pages for all expansions including Onslaught 3, alternate maps, neutral unit packs, and Great Old One packs
  - Free (browsing)

The podcast host specifically recommends Gray Wizard Gaming in Episode 1: "shout out Gray Wizard Gaming, who, as I was learning to play this game myself, I watched every single video that he made. They're all great." This channel is the primary video tutorial resource for Cthulhu Wars.

## Online Play

### Tabletop Simulator (Steam)

The Opening the Way podcast community organizes games on Tabletop Simulator, which has a Cthulhu Wars mod available on the Steam Workshop. This is the primary platform for online play in the community.

- **Tabletop Simulator on Steam**
  [https://store.steampowered.com/app/286160/Tabletop_Simulator/](https://store.steampowered.com/app/286160/Tabletop_Simulator/)
  - Type: PC game / board game simulator
  - Cost: Paid (Tabletop Simulator itself costs ~$20; the Cthulhu Wars mod is community-created and free once you own TTS)
  - The Cthulhu Wars mod on the Steam Workshop includes all expansions and maps
